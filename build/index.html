Is It Vegan?

The goal of this application is to help you determine if there are ingredients in your food item that are not vegan.
Some ingredients can be made from both vegan and non-vegan sources, depending on the manufacture of the ingredient.
When a UPC is entered two lists will be displayed. One list of ingredients that are not vegan and one list that might not be vegan.
